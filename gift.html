Gift Membership FAQ

Gift recipients receive a beautiful email with their gift certificate and redemption instructions. You can schedule delivery for a specific date or send immediately. Physical certificates and welcome boxes are mailed separately via tracked shipping.

Yes! Gift recipients can exchange for different membership tiers or durations. They can also choose to apply the value toward any available membership option. Exchange requests must be made within 30 days of redemption.

If the recipient is already a member, the gift value can be applied to extend their current membership, upgrade to a higher tier, or added as credit toward future membership renewals and event tickets.

Gift memberships must be redeemed within 12 months of purchase. We send reminder emails to both the giver and recipient before the expiration date to ensure no gifts go unused.
