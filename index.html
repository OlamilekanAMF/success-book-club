Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining our book club

How do I join the book club?

Simply sign up for free using your email address. You'll receive our monthly book picks and access to member discussions.

Are the book discussions virtual?

Yes! We host monthly virtual discussions via video call. Past recordings are available for members who can't attend live.

Do I need to buy the books?

While we recommend purchasing books for the best experience, members can also access audio versions through our partners.

Can I suggest a book for the club?

Absolutely! We love member suggestions. Submit your recommendations through the community page each month.
