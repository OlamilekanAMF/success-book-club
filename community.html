Community Guidelines

Help us create a safe, welcoming space for all readers

Be Respectful

Honor diverse opinions and experiences. Treat all members with kindness and consideration, even when you disagree with their perspective.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions focused on books, reading experiences, and themes of triumph. Avoid unrelated topics and self-promotion.

No Spoilers Without Warning

Use spoiler warnings when discussing plot details. Not everyone has read every book, so help preserve the reading experience for others.

Share Thoughtfully

Contribute valuable insights rather than just agreeing. Bring your unique perspective to enrich the conversation for everyone.
