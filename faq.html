Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Success Book Club

Membership & Subscription

As a member, you'll receive a carefully selected book each month featuring inspiring stories of triumph and resilience. Members also get access to exclusive author events, community discussions, and our private forum.

We offer three membership tiers: Digital ($9.99/month) includes digital books and community access, Standard ($19.99/month) includes physical books and all digital benefits, and Premium ($29.99/month) includes everything plus exclusive author meet-and-greets.

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. Your benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period, and you'll keep any books you've already received.

Book Selections

Our team reads hundreds of books each year and selects titles that feature compelling stories of triumph, resilience, and human achievement. We focus on diverse voices and genres that inspire and empower our readers.

While our selections are curated, we love hearing from our members! You can suggest books through our community forum or contact page. We consider member feedback when planning future selections.

If you already own the monthly selection, you can choose a credit toward any book in our collection or swap for an alternate inspirational title from our curated list.

Shipping & Delivery

We currently ship to all 50 US states and Canada. International shipping is available to select countries for an additional fee. Digital memberships are available worldwide.

Physical books are shipped on the 1st of each month and typically arrive within 5-7 business days. Digital books are available for download on the 1st of each month.

Contact us within 14 days of expected delivery and we'll immediately send a replacement copy or issue a full refund. We'll also investigate any shipping issues with our carrier.

Community & Events

Members receive automatic invitations to all author events via email. Events are held monthly and you can register through the links in your email or through our events page.

Yes! All author events are recorded and available to members in our community forum within 48 hours of the live event.

Join our private community forum where you can discuss books, share insights, participate in reading challenges, and connect with fellow book lovers who share your passion for inspiring stories.

Billing & Payments

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership tier at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any price differences.

Absolutely! Gift memberships are available in 3, 6, and 12-month options. You can purchase them through our gift memberships page and schedule delivery for a special occasion.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help! Contact our friendly support team.
